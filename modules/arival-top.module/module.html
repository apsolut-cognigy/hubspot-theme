How to create a booking website ?

  • Obtain a good domain name

    Choose a name that is easy to spell, short to remember, avoid numbers and hyphens, make sure that your website domain is unique.

  • Prominently display a clear description of your business

    It is important to let people understand in a sentence who you are and what you are doing. Make your homepage recognizable, with a catchy image, key words on your activity.

  • Implement the best content management system

    There are many content management system but the only one entirely adapted to your industry, is the Regiondo website Builder - Ask for a demo here.

  • Create an interesting, memorable, and engaging website user interface

    Use beautiful graphics, if you write on the image, make sure the font is easy to read. Compress and optimize every image, otherwise your website will take too long to load. Stay consistent on your brand, use 2-3 colour, and stick to it.

  • Optimize your small business website for search engines

    SEO is the north star you need to reach, it takes time to be successful, but when it's done, bookings will flood in.

  • Regularly create and publish quality content

    Google loves consistency, and users love novelty, this is why creating and publish content will help you to keep your network interesting, and Google will rank you in the top research.
